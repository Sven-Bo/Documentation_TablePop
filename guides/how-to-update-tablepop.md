# How to update TablePop

This guide shows you how to replace your current TablePop add-in with a newer version. The process takes about one minute.

### Written Steps

1. **Open Excel**
2. **Go to Excel Add-ins**\
   Click File\
   Click Options\
   Click Add-ins

At the bottom of the window, make sure Manage is set to Excel Add-ins.\
Click Go.

3. **Deactivate the current TablePop add-in**\
   In the list of add-ins, find TablePop.\
   Untick the checkbox next to it.\
   Click OK.
4. **Add the new TablePop version**\
   Go again to File\
   Options\
   Add-ins\
   Click Go.

Click Browse.\
Select the new TablePop add-in file you just downloaded.

If Excel asks whether you want to replace the existing file, click Replace.

5. **Confirm and finish**\
   Click OK.\
   Click OK again if prompted.

That is it. TablePop is now updated and ready to use.
